## Overview

**ManuDocs** is a production-ready, AI-driven **global trade automation platform** designed to digitize and unify the complete import–export ecosystem.

Unlike traditional tools that focus only on documentation, ManuDocs acts as a **single digital interface** connecting buyers, sellers, manufacturers, exporters, importers, logistics partners, customs authorities, shipping lines, airlines, and banks.

---

## What ManuDocs Automates

- Buyer and seller discovery  
- Product intelligence and HS code mapping  
- Manufacturing and export readiness workflows  
- Import and export documentation  
- DGFT, ICEGATE, and customs compliance  
- Freight forwarding and logistics coordination  
- Shipping line and airline workflows  
- Banking, payments, and trade finance processes  
- Audit trails, reporting, and compliance records  

**From factory floor to foreign port.  
From buyer onboarding to bank settlement.**

## Platform Vision

> ManuDocs aims to become the **Unified Digital Trade Interface** for international commerce — replacing fragmented, paper-driven workflows with a single AI-powered digital system.
